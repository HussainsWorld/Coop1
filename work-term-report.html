I was employed at the Registrar's Office which is located at the 3rd floor of the University Centre. A major thing we discussed is the project of merging our databases from the Fall and Winter semesters.

Job Description

My role was titled Scheduling Support, and I performed a variety of programming, software development, and data analysis duties in my employment. Finding and fixing differences in instructor IDs between semesters, like the fall and winter terms, was one of the most fascinating assignments I worked on. I used the JNDI API and Java Apache POI to tackle this, which made it possible for me to effectively communicate with Excel files and directory services for LDAP lookups. I taught myself how to do the Java analysis on the job, however, concerning my data manipulation using Python and Pandas, that was something I learned previously through personal projects such as my Movie Recommendation System.

I learned what it means to work as a part of a team and developed my proficiency in various technologies such as Python and Java while doing so. I will be here for the fall term and I'm excited to continue to learn more.
